Visualising data of Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin also holds the record of most number of runs ever scored in a calendar year. He achieved the feat in 1998 where he scored a total of 1894 runs.
Managing a career average of 44.83 over a span of more than 20 years is a monumental achievement in itself. His average peaked in 2001 when he scored at 69.54 per innings.
Sachin also has a record of getting out the most number of times in the 90s. A clear pattern emerges that there's a cluster of 90s scores between his 350th-450th innings.
