A Global Phenomenon

Visualising data of Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin has scored more than 50% of his runs away from home which defines his class. He has scored the most number of runs in UAE ( Remember the desert storms at Sharjah? ) followed by Sri Lanka and Australia.
Sachin has an average of above 30 in almost all the major countries that he has played in. Zimbabwe happens to be the country where he averages the highest.
Out of the 49 ODI centuries, 29 of them have come outside India.
The graph clearly shows that India went on to win the match 70% of times when Sachin has scored a century.
